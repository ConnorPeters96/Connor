Welcome to Computing at Edge Hill University

A degree with computing will give you the skills and know-how to proceed onto the next step in life and attaining the opportunities available in this 'digital age'. Here, you will be learning how to produce and develop technologies, alongside making the most use out of technologies that already exist. We offer you the opportunities to choose what you would like to specialise in, and the pathways you would like to move into. Teaching and learning techniques, as well as staff who constantly offer support and have won awards for excellence, give an upbeat and enjoyable, but effective learning environment. Learning to work as part of a team on real-life projects using leading-edge resources and industry-standard software will enable you to develop a breadth of practical and communication skills and give you an excellent skill set to pursue a wide range of careers with a distinct workplace advantage.
